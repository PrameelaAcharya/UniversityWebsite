BMS College of Engineering

Established in 1946, B.M.S. College of Engineering (BMSCE) is the first private sector engineering initiative in India and is located in the heart of Bengaluru. It is an autonomous institution affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), consistently ranking as a top-tier technical college with an A++ NAAC accreditation.
